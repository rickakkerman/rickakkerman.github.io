Rick Akkerman

I am a research intern at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (MPI-IS) in Tübingen, advised by Dimitris Tzionas, Victoria Fernández Abrevaya and Haiwen Feng.

At Google I've worked on Glass, Lens Blur, HDR+, VR, Portrait Mode, Portrait Light, and Maps. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I've received the PAMI Young Researcher Award.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, generative AI, and image processing. Most of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, motion, color, light, etc) from images, usually with radiance fields.
